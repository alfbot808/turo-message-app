# Hostly - Demo Script

## Recording Setup
- Use your phone, screen record on
- Start on the Messages tab
- Have at least one test trip added (status: picked_up, return date: tomorrow)

---

## Opening (5 seconds)
**[Show Messages tab, scroll slightly]**

"This is Hostly — the tool I built for Turo hosts. I have two cars — a Tesla and a Hyundai — at two locations, airport and Kaneohe. Problem is, I kept sending the wrong info to guests. Tesla checkout instructions to Hyundai guests, airport directions to Kaneohe pickups..."

---

## Feature 1: Generate Message (15 seconds)
**[Tap Tesla → Airport → Pickup Day]**

"So I pick the car, the location, the stage of the trip. I type the guest name..."

**[Type "John" in Guest Name, tap Generate Message]**

"...and it pulls the exact template I need. One tap to copy, paste into Turo. Done."

**[Tap Copy button]**

---

## Feature 2: Trip Tracker (20 seconds)
**[Switch to Trip Tracker tab]**

"Here's where I track all my active trips. I can see at a glance: who's booked, who's out with a car, who needs a review."

**[Point to checkout warning if visible]**

"This is the killer feature — when a trip is ending within 24 hours, I get this warning. And I can tap 'Send Checkout'..."

**[Tap Send Checkout button]**

"...and it jumps me back to Messages with the checkout template pre-filled. Right car, right location, guest name already there."

---

## Feature 3: FAQ (10 seconds)
**[Scroll down to FAQ section]**

"I also get the same questions over and over. Where's the lockbox? How do I start the Tesla? I can tap any of these, copy the answer, done."

**[Tap a FAQ, show the generated response]**

---

## Closing (5 seconds)
**[Back to top, show both tabs]**

"That's it. No more wrong messages, no more typing the same thing 20 times a week. Just the right info, right guest, right time."

---

## Total Time: ~55 seconds
